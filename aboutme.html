About Me

Hello, I'm Jessie - it's nice to meet you!

I'm a new graduate from Brandeis International Business School with a master's degree in Business Analytics. My expertise lies in data analysis, project management, and building predictive models using machine learning algorithms in Python.
Before joining Brandeis University, I earned my bachelor's degree in logistics management at BTBU in Beijing, China, where I received the Outstanding Graduate Award. During my senior year, I participated in an exchange program in Ireland, which gave me the chance to work with advanced statistical modeling techniques and modern analytical tools.


I have a passion for problem-solving and a drive for continuous learning and improvement. In my free time, I take online courses to keep up with the latest technology and industry trends. Recently, I passed the AWS ML specialty certificate exam and feel confident in building cloud-based applications in the workplace. I also listen to podcasts about recent industry news.

With all the tools and techniques I've learned so far, I'm ready to take on more challenging tasks and contribute to the workplace by diving into real-world problems. I enjoy collaborating with others and sharing my ideas and additional resources with the whole team. One of my teammates once told me that she learned a lot from our discussions and appreciated my ability to motivate others. That kind of positive feedback is very rewarding for me.

Thank you for taking the time to learn a bit about me. I look forward to connecting and collaborating with you!
